About Me

I was born In Port-Au-Prince Haiti on March 18 1987, the Oldest of seven kids. I lived in Haiti for the majority of my life until the 1993 coup d'etat when my family was force to flee the nation due to rising violence. From the age of 12 to 18 I lived in Columbia Maryland where I attended Middle school and high school. After graduating highschool I attended Full Sail University and Joined the United States Miliary. Ater 3 years of service I moved to Nashville TN where I now reside with my beatiful wife and daugther.
